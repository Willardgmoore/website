Holiday Rambler Online Privacy Policy

These guidelines and privacy policy are specific to websites managed by Allied Recreation Group, Inc., and its subsidiaries (including but not limited to the Holiday Rambler division; collectively referred to as "Holiday Rambler", "We" or "Us"). The guidelines and privacy policy are effective August 1, 2011, and apply to information gathered through these websites and via e-mail.

Holiday Rambler maintains strict privacy policies to safeguard personal identifying information and communications from unauthorized intrusions. By "personal identifiable information", We mean any personal information that identifies you, such as name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, and any information about activities (online or otherwise) that is directly linked to your information.

How and Why Holiday Rambler Collects Your Information

As you navigate through our website, at certain points, we may need to collect personal identifying information from you in order to provide you with services tailored to your needs.

  • Holiday Rambler will not disclose any personal information collected from your activities on any Holiday Rambler online service except to Holiday Rambler agents, dealers and marketing partners.
  • When Holiday Rambler uses agents, such as contractors or companies to perform services on Our behalf, or provides your information to Holiday Rambler dealers or marketing partners, We will use reasonable commercial efforts to ensure that the company protects your personal information in a manner consistent with this Policy.

Holiday Rambler will collect and use your information only for specific purposes. Holiday Rambler will collect and use personal information:

  • to work with marketing partners to bring goods and services that may be of interest to you;
  • to create and inform you of products and services that better meet your needs;
  • for billing purposes;
  • to anticipate and/or resolve problems with your service;
  • to provide referrals to our dealers for further follow-up for services or products that We think may be of interest to you.

You may choose not to receive any marketing communications from Holiday Rambler in connection with our online services. You may also tell Us how you would like to be contacted in connection with information you provided to Us via our online services. If you notify Us that you do not want to receive any marketing information, We:

  • will not contact you directly with marketing messages in connection with Holiday Rambler online services;
  • will not use personal information obtained from your online registration or use of a Holiday Rambler online service to contact you with marketing messages about any products or services;
  • will not disclose your information to others.

To inform Us of your preferred method of communication or to indicate that you do or do not wish to receive communications from Holiday Rambler, Holiday Rambler dealers or our marketing partners in connection with information you provided on this site, please register here.

If you notify Us that you do not want Us to use any of your information in the ways described in this Policy, We will honor your request. However, if you then later provide additional personal information to this site or otherwise respond to one of Our online inquiries, We will assume that you have changed your mind with respect to Our use of your information, and We may contact you as described herein. You may at anytime, of course, follow the procedure described above to elect not to be contacted by Us.

Personal information that a visitor volunteers at one of the Holiday Rambler websites to inquire about or order Holiday Rambler products/services will be protected just as if the information had been provided under more traditional ways of ordering that product or service.

Holiday Rambler will use commercially reasonable efforts to assure that your personal information will be kept secure. Holiday Rambler has implemented technology and security features and strict policy guidelines to safeguard the privacy of your personal information from unauthorized access or improper use, and we will continue to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available.

Holiday Rambler will use commercially reasonable efforts to assure that your e-mail will be kept private. Holiday Rambler will not read or disclose to those parties not affiliated with Holiday Rambler, private e-mail communications that are transmitted using Holiday Rambler services, except as required to operate the service or as otherwise required by law.

Holiday Rambler will respond to improper conduct. Holiday Rambler may use personal information to investigate and help prevent potentially unlawful activity or activity that threatens the company or otherwise violates the customer agreement for any products or services that Holiday Rambler provides.

We may use session log data and cookies combined with anonymous user data (and personal data if you provide it) to track user movement and behavior within and across this website. We may use this information to help Us or our authorized dealers to offer to you Holiday Rambler products and services that may serve your needs and to perform website-user analytics. We may supply this information to a third party metric provider to perform the analysis for Us and to provide the results to Us. Our relationship with the metric provider is governed by contract. The contract restricts the metric provider's use of the data to performing requested analytics and compiling reports for Holiday Rambler. The metric provider is prohibited from otherwise selling or sharing the information or the results with anyone else. We use the information and the analytic results to improve our website and the user's experience.

Changes in Practices

Holiday Rambler will keep its privacy policy current, and make every effort to promptly update this web page every time there is a change to this policy.

Additional Privacy Protections for Children Using the Internet

Holiday Rambler recognizes that children, including young teens, may not be able to make informed choices about personal information requested online. Accordingly, Holiday Rambler does not knowingly target children or teenagers (younger than eighteen years of age) for collection of information online. Holiday Rambler does not solicit or collect personally identifying information from children and teenagers under eighteen and does not allow anyone else to do so on any Holiday Rambler website.
