Full Service RV Dealer Serving Denver, CO.
Germaine RV Sales and Renovators is a full service dealer offering new and used RVs for sale. Our dealership is located in Mesa, Arizona proudly serving Denver, Colorado. Our location carries a variety of new and used RVs, travel trailers, fifth wheels, motorhomes and more for sale. We are proud to be a dealer that carries travel trailers and fifth wheels from the top brand names you know and trust such as Komfort, Host and Max Lite here in Arizona. In addition to offering new and used recreational vehicle sales, our dealership offers service. We are a dealer that has highly professional and efficient technicians here to help with repairs, yearly maintenance, and customized work. If you want to sell your recreational vehicle, we buy, sell and offer consignment. Give us a call at 888-766-4703 or email us and one of our sales representatives will be happy to serve your recreational vehicle needs.
